AutoBuddys

We provide an efficient, cost effective solution for the automated monitoring of autistic and elderly individuals.

About Our Project

AutoBuddys Project is divided into 3 different products -
The Educative Development Game for Autistic Children and the Health Monitoring and Fall Detection device for the Elderly People.

Our Objectives

We at AutoBuddys strive to develop an innovative solution that can create an impact in the lives of many.
We love to care about you.

Interactive Use

A visually engaging and adapted game for children with autism

Amplifying Efficiency

Incorporating active involvement of individuals with fun learning

Prompt Assistance

Detection of anomalous activities for elderly individuals

Analysis and Tracking

Easy-to-use dashboard for monitoring the progress

Our Features

We are only focused on providing the greatest service and making the health monitoring procedure as simple as possible.

One Click Away Our health monitoring system can provide you a seamless experience for keeping your health in check.
Your health guardian Measure your vitals with one device anytime anywhere with a battery that does not give up and always has your back.
Fun and Engaging Blend in this world of colourful gaming where the kid can play, also allowing the caretaker to monitor the child's progress.
Interact and Improve Enhance your child's Communication skills with our app which interprets the speech patterns and also captures the child's attention span.
